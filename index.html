About Us

We Offer best program for Shaping the best Future

Meppayur Salafi College of Teacher Education was established in 1995 as a professional teacher training college dedicated to the development of competent, committed, and socially responsible educators. Since its inception, the college has been providing quality teacher education with a strong focus on academic excellence, ethical values, and professional competence.

The institution has a rich academic tradition and a distinguished history in the field of teacher education. The college is recognized by the Government of Kerala, affiliated to the University of Calicut, and approved by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).
